Community Guidelines

Keep it a good place

01
Respect everyone. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks. Treat others the way you want to be treated.
02
No spam or self-promotion. Don't flood chats or promote your own content without permission.
03
Stay on-topic. Use the right channels for the right content so everyone can find what they need.
04
No NSFW content. Keep all content appropriate for all members at all times.
05
Listen to the staff. Moderators have the final say. If you have an issue, open a support ticket — don't argue publicly.
06
No cheating in tournaments. Fair play is everything. Cheaters will be banned and disqualified immediately.
07
Have fun. That's what we're here for. Welcome to Amader Elaka! 🎉
Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before joining the Elaka.

Is Amader Elaka free to join? +
Yes! Joining the server is completely free. Discord itself is free to use, and we don't charge any membership fees. Just click the Join button and you're in.
What games do you host tournaments for? +
We regularly host tournaments for Free Fire and PUBG Mobile. We're always looking to expand, so if there's a game you want to see added, suggest it in our feedback channel!
How do I get the Member role? +
Once you join the server, head to the #welcome channel and follow the instructions to accept the rules and pick your roles. You'll be a verified member in under a minute.
Can I apply to be a Staff member? +
Yes! When staff applications are open, we post an announcement in #announcements. Keep an eye on that channel, be an active and positive community member, and you'll have a great shot.
What happens if someone breaks the rules? +
Our moderation team follows a clear strike system: warning → mute → kick → ban, depending on severity. Serious offences like cheating or harassment result in an immediate permanent ban.
